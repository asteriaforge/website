Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Asteria Forge SAS ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our contact form.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Name: Your full name
  • Email Address: Your contact email
  • Subject: The subject of your inquiry
  • Message: Your message content

We do not collect any information automatically through cookies or tracking technologies on this website. This website is designed for company presentation, product information, and direct contact.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide through our contact form solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide customer support
  • Send you requested information about our products
  • Follow up on your message

We will never use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Data Protection

Your data is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmitted through our contact form is encrypted using industry-standard security protocols.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and fulfill our legal obligations. Typically, this is retained for a period of one (1) year unless required longer by law.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used by Asteria Forge team members to respond to your inquiry.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: info@asteriaforge.com
